When I was freaking out my skin care routine something that really helped me was finding information. Some of the resources that I found really helpful were communities on Reddit. The reason why I found read Reddit a very useful resource it was because I was able to get tips and advice from people first hand. Additionally since posts can reach many people I was able to find out differing opinions and tips for finding a derm. I found a sense of community on the skin care page and I felt like I was able to have a sense of camaraderie going into the skin care process as a newbie. Something that I would advise against is taking information as a fact online. The pages I used were nice for basic skin care like cleansers and how to find derms which did not cover medical concerns. Even for basic skin care opinions vary so don't take on person's idea as a fact. For medical questions try to seek a doctor.
